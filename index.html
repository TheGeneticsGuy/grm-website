Why Use GRM?

Designed by a Guild Leader, for Guild Leaders.

Better Guild Logging

Track joins, leaves, kicks, promotions, demotions, level ups, inactivity, note changes, birthdays, member anniversaries, and even name changes. Know exactly who did what and when.

Alt/Main Management

Identify alts and group them into an alt group with the ability to set 1 toon of the alt group as "Main." Mouseover any player in the roster to see their Main, their rank history, and all grouped alts.

Ban List System

Prevents banned players from sneaking back in. GRM alerts you if a banned player rejoins. Detects name-changes using GUID tracking (Permanent ID) to catch banned players even if they change their name.

Secure Data Syncing

Decentralized database syncing keeps officers on the same page. Syncs Mains, Alts, Bans, Join Date, Rank Promotion Dates, and Custom Notes. Includes granular trust restrictions so only authorized ranks can sync critical data.

Macro Tool

Membership History

GRM stores the complete history of a player, including original join dates, leave dates, and rejoin dates. Tracks the date of every promotion (or demotion) they have ever received, not just the last one.
